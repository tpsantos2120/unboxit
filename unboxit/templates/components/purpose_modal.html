Purpose

The purpose of this website is to empower users to build their own watchlist library of TV Shows and Movies. It could be something you have already watched or something that is yet to be released.


The main features we provide to registered members:

  • Search for Movies and TV Shows
  • Get random recommend Movies or TV Shows
  • Get the lastest trending Movies

If you are not a registered member you can still search for TV shows and Movies, but you will not be able to build a watchlist, get recommended titles and get treding Movies.
